The additional printed parts to make a hydro cooled Mine4Heat single board S19.

Buy an aluminum enclosure (or get the 3d print files) from https://mine4heat.com/

In addition to the enclosure and printed parts, you'll also need an S19 hashboard (I used s19j Pro), the correct water block for your hashboard (CryoByte or Zeus Mining), control board, power supply (APW12 or APW3++), hydro reservoir/pump, 240mm radiator, 2x 120mm fans, tubing, hydro fittings, and wiring.

You'll need to manually remove part of the extended grille to fit your water block ports through (I just cut in the right spot with some flush cutters).
